Software Testing Overview

Testing is a critical phase of Software Development Life Cycle. Manual testing is the process in which the defects are identified, isolated, subjected for justification and ensure that the product is defect-free, in-order to produce quality product. However, it requires the necessary knowledge such as about different types of manual testing, software development life cycle.
